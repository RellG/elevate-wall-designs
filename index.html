— 04 / Process

Five steps, from
idea to install.

A calm, transparent process — free estimate, samples and palette brought to your door, soft panels manufactured in Fort Lauderdale, and installed by our own crew. Most projects move from estimate to installed in two to six weeks.

i.

Book a Free Estimate

Send us your dimensions or schedule a complimentary on-site visit at a time that works for you, anywhere in South Florida.

ii.

Samples Come to You

Our team arrives with physical panel samples and the full color palette so you can choose against your actual light, walls, and furniture.

iii.

Measure & Quote

We take precise measurements on site and follow up with a fixed, all-in quote. No hidden line items, no upsell pressure.

iv.

Built in Fort Lauderdale

Soft panels are hand-built to your exact dimensions in our Fort Lauderdale manufacturing facility.

v.

Professional Install

Our own install crew arrives on time, protects your space, and leaves it spotless. One to two days for most rooms.
